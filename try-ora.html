Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Ora?

Getting started with Ora is simple. Just sign up for a free account, download the app on your preferred device, and follow the onboarding process. You'll be guided through setting up your profile and connecting any devices or services you want to integrate with Ora.

Is my data secure with Ora?

Absolutely. Ora is built with privacy and security as core principles. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and you have complete control over what is collected and how it's used. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What devices does Ora work with?

Ora works with most popular smartphones, smartwatches, fitness trackers, and other wellness devices. This includes Apple devices, Android phones, Fitbit, Garmin, Oura Ring, and many more. We're constantly adding support for new devices and platforms.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the free Basic plan.
