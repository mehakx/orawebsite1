Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support issues, Premium and Ultimate subscribers receive priority response times.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

Yes, we offer customized enterprise solutions for organizations looking to support employee wellness at scale. Please contact our sales team at enterprise@ora.ai for more information about our enterprise plans and pricing.

How can I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs or technical issues through our contact form by selecting "Technical Support" as the subject. Please include as much detail as possible, including your device model, operating system version, and steps to reproduce the issue.

Are you open to partnerships or integrations?

Absolutely! We're always interested in exploring partnerships with complementary services and device manufacturers. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form and provide details about your organization and partnership ideas.
